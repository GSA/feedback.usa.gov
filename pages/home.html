What Feedback Analytics offers



The Feedback Analytics Program

The Feedback Analytics Program develops and manages government-wide services that collect and report quantitative and qualitative customer feedback to aid service improvement and decision-making. This includes methods to evaluate progress toward achieving customer experience goals and inform continual improvements to service design and delivery.

Assessing public service delivery

Helping grow the maturity of Customer Experience in federal government organization. Sharing best practices on customer experience practices and tools.

Touchpoints

Touchpoints is a GSA developed and maintained customer experience feedback tool. Touchpoints leverages customer feedback as a strategic asset; informing continuous service design.
